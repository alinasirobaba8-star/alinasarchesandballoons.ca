About Me

Hi, I’m Alina — the creator behind Alina’s Arches & Balloons, a luxury décor service based in Montreal.

What started as a creative passion quickly turned into something much bigger. I’ve always been inspired by beautiful details, soft color palettes, and the way décor can completely transform a space.

I specialize in custom balloon installations, small customized event details, and elegant floral arches for weddings, birthdays, baby showers, bridal showers, gender reveals, and special events.

Every setup is designed with care, creativity, and attention to detail to bring each client’s vision to life in a refined and unforgettable way.
